Our Story

Ooty Tourism is dedicated to promoting the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam. Our journey began with a passion for showcasing the breathtaking landscapes, rich biodiversity, and vibrant culture of this hill station nestled in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India.

Founded by a team of travel enthusiasts, nature lovers, and local experts, Ooty Tourism aims to provide visitors with authentic and memorable experiences that capture the essence of this enchanting destination.

Our Mission

At Ooty Tourism, our mission is to create unforgettable memories for travelers while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit the local community and preserve the natural environment.
