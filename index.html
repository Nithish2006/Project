Explore the Beauty of Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station in Tamil Nadu, India. It is famous for its lush green landscapes, serene lakes, and picturesque tea gardens. Explore the mesmerizing beauty of Ooty with us!
