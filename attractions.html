Ooty Botanical Gardens

The Ooty Botanical Gardens is a lush and well-maintained garden located at the lower slopes of Doddabetta Peak. It houses a variety of exotic and indigenous plants, trees, and flowers.

Doddabetta Peak

Doddabetta Peak is the highest mountain peak in the Nilgiri Hills and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. It is a popular spot for trekking and picnics.

Ooty Lake

Ooty Lake is an artificial lake constructed in the early 19th century. Visitors can enjoy boating, fishing, and horse riding around the lake, making it a favorite destination for families.
